Bakemonogatari

Aagaman Luitel | words: 359 | 1 minutes | 2024.09.27
manga

I have watched every monogatari series but only read ~30ish chapters of bakemonogatari since I thought its same as anime. But oh boy I was wrong. As much as like the series I loved reading the bakemonogatari manga more.

Bake made me fall in love characters. Every character has their own funny and adorable traits. Lets go with something lighthearted, Kanbaru. Kanbaru story is not heavy but she is wayy damn cute. Her appearence and jokes are wonderfully done. Another one is Hachikuji. She and Rararagi’s conversations are just funny to read. On a heavy note, Hanekawa. Boy this story made me depressed and motivated at same time. Hanekawa story is one of the best story I’ve ever read. I just feel sad and happy. Being released from a trap in an imperfect way you grow, suffer, cry, act, you hide your emotions, you want someone to say they care for you, you want to exist in someone’s world. I have seen lots of scene where girls gets rejected and cuts her hair at end but this was more than that. The love story is beautifully done. This manga gave me hope, love, hate, despair and motivation. It has seemingly grown inside of me. I feel indebted to this story. It has changed how I think, how I should act and most of all what I should expect from someone and be ready to accept it after all.
