Prison school

2023-03-17

So I finished prison school and it was my worst decision.

I still can't get over that Chiyo is old Miyo now.

I wished Hana won which she technically did but not like this.

I had a feeling if Hana won Chiyo would be sad but what Hana and Kiyoshi did Chiyo in her birthday broke her.

That last panel will haunt me forever. Thanks Akira Hiramoto.

Will I read next manga Raw Hero by Akira Hiramoto which I presume made him rush prison school to the point its ending is like a plane disappearing in midway flight? YES I WILL.

I just hope I don't get addicted and have high hopes for it.
